### 5 Do's for College Students:



* &#x20;   Do adjust your privacy settings: Regularly check who can see your posts and personal information.



* &#x20;   Do showcase your projects: Use platforms like LinkedIn to highlight your academic work and coding challenges.



* &#x20;   Do think before you post: Remember that the internet is permanent; assume future employers will see it.



* &#x20;   Do verify information: Check facts before sharing or retweeting news to avoid spreading misinformation.



* &#x20;   Do engage respectfully: Treat online discussions with the same professionalism as a classroom debate.





### 5 Don'ts for College Students:



* &#x20;   Don't overshare personal details: Never post your phone number, address, or sensitive financial information.



* &#x20;   Don't post emotional rants: Avoid venting about professors, peers, or college administration online.



* &#x20;   Don't use offensive language: Keep your tone appropriate and respectful across all public platforms.



* &#x20;   Don't ignore copyright: Don't claim others' work, images, or code as your own without proper credit.



* &#x20;   Don't engage with trolls: Avoid getting drawn into heated, unproductive arguments in comment sections.
